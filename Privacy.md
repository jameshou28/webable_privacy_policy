# WebAble Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 12/29/25

---

## Data Collection
WebAble collects only user accessibility preferences, including:

- Text size settings
- Font preferences (including dyslexic-friendly font choice)
- Text and line spacing preferences
- High contrast mode settings
### Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage
No data is transmitted to external servers
No data is collected or stored by third parties
### Data Usage
Your settings are used solely to maintain your accessibility preferences across browser sessions
Settings are automatically applied when you visit websites to ensure consistent accessibility experience
### Data Sharing
We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties
Your accessibility preferences remain completely private and local to your device
### Data Security
All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms
No personal identifiers, browsing history, or sensitive information is collected
